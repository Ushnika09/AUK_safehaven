Safety Tips

Expert advice to help you stay safe

Traveling Alone

Always share your itinerary with a trusted friend or family member. Use our SafeCheck feature to send automatic updates.

Night Safety

Stay in well-lit areas and consider using our virtual companion feature for added security when walking at night.

Public Transport

Sit near the driver or conductor and always keep your personal belongings secure and within sight.
